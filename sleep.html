ABOUT GENTLE LOVE.

Saxophonist Norihiko Hibino (Metal Gear Solid/Bayonetta series) and pianist AYAKI (Etrian Odyssey/PersonaQ), known as GENTLE LOVE when performing together, have toured throughout Japan, bringing their unique therapeutic approach to music to venues big and small. Their Prescription for Sleep: Game Music Lullabies series is streamed millions of times every year, has garnered awards in the videogame industry, and has charted on Billboard’s jazz and new age charts.
